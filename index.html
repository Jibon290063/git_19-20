History of GitHub: GitHub was founded in 2008 by Tom Preston-Werner, Chris Wanstrath, and PJ Hyett. It quickly became one of the most popular platforms for hosting and collaborating on software projects. GitHub allows developers to share their code, track changes, and collaborate with others around the world. It has played a significant role in the open-source software movement and has become an essential tool for developers of all levels.
